Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide through conversations with our AI matchmaker, including personal details, values, preferences, and lifestyle information used for compatibility matching. We also collect your email address and basic demographic information provided at registration.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to facilitate introductions between compatible members. We use AI analysis to assess compatibility and suggest one match at a time. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your profile data is stored securely. Photos are stored on our cloud storage provider. Conversation history is retained to improve your matching experience.

Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time using the "Cancel Account" option in settings or the footer of any page. Upon deletion, your profile, conversations, and photos are permanently removed.

Terms of Service

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

Eligibility

You must be 18 years of age or older to use Love-Matcher. By creating an account you confirm that you are single and not in a committed relationship.

One Match at a Time

Love-Matcher operates on a one-match-at-a-time model. You will receive a single introduction at a time. You may decline and return to the pool at any time.

Conduct

You agree to engage honestly and respectfully. Providing false information in your profile is grounds for account termination. Harassment of other members is strictly prohibited.

Service

Love-Matcher is currently free for all members. We make no guarantees about match timing or outcomes. The service is provided as-is.

Termination

You may delete your account at any time. We reserve the right to terminate accounts that violate these terms.

Delete Your Account

Are you sure? This will permanently delete your profile, conversation history, and all data. This cannot be undone.
